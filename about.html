About Beyond Grades

Empowering students to discover their voice, purpose, and confidence.

Our Mission

At Beyond Grades, we believe college admissions should celebrate who students are — not just what they score. In today’s test-optional era, authenticity, reflection, and purpose matter more than ever. Our mission is to help students move beyond transcripts and test scores to uncover the story, passions, and purpose that make them shine.

From building meaningful extracurriculars to crafting essays that speak from the heart, our counselors guide students with empathy, structure, and insight. We focus on more than college acceptance — we help young people develop clarity, confidence, and direction that will carry them through college and life beyond.

Our Core Values

  • Authenticity: We help students express their true selves through honest, reflective storytelling.
  • Growth: Every challenge is a chance to learn, not just a step toward admission.
  • Balance: We emphasize well-being alongside academic and extracurricular success.
  • Empowerment: Our goal is to build independent thinkers and confident communicators.

About the Founder

Evan Huang is the Director of Administration at TECC Academy and CFO of the TECC Foundation, located in the heart of the San Gabriel Valley. A Temple City native, Evan understands firsthand the academic and emotional pressures local students face.

Inspired by his own experiences growing up in this community, Evan founded Beyond Grades to help students find their unique voice and stand out in a test-optional admissions world. His leadership background — from serving as an Eagle Scout and AYSO referee to mentoring others in law enforcement and education — reflects his lifelong commitment to service and growth.

Evan earned his B.S. in Criminal Justice and Criminology from California State University, Long Beach (Cum Laude) and a Master of Studies in Law from the University of Southern California, specializing in compliance and business law. Through Beyond Grades, he combines mentorship, education, and leadership to empower students to define success on their own terms.

Our Team

Our consultants include former U.S. college admissions officers, SAT instructors, and essay specialists. Together, we provide comprehensive, end-to-end guidance — from planning and profile building to interviews and final decisions.
